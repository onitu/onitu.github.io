You are using lots of different web services ? You have files everywhere and struggle to keep them synchronized ? Install Onitu and take the control back over your data !

Download on Github

Your favorite services, together

With Onitu you can use all your services (Dropbox, Drive, Evernote…) and share any data between them. You want to save your notes from Evernote on your FTP server ? No problem ! You want to edit your Dropbox files with Google Docs ? Easy peasy. You want to put your laptop photos on Flickr ? Do it in a breeze.

Your files, your rules

Orchestrate your files with specific rules for them. You can say where your files go according to their type, their size, their name, their path… For example you can send your small Videos to Dropbox, the big ones to Hubic, and backup all of them to your FTP server.

Install it anywhere

Onitu is a Free Software that you can install on any computer. It's a community-driven project, there is no company behind it willing to steal your data or spy on you. You can connect all your computers with our client.

All your files in one place

With the Web interface, you can see the files you have in your different services, interact with them and control Onitu.
